OUR STORY

Salted Seas was founded on the belief that clean, science-backed skincare should be accessible to everyone. Drawing on the ocean’s mineral-rich resources, we partnered with expert formulators to develop high-performance products powered by Dead Sea salt, marine botanicals, and clinically proven actives. From the start, we’ve committed to delivering premium results without unnecessary fillers or premium prices — making transformative skincare as restorative as the sea itself.
