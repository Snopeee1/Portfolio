From the Barbershop

Two Brothers Grooming Co. started where most real ideas begin — in a garage and behind the barber chair. What began as Evilfades Barbershop evolved into a mission to create grooming products that actually perform in the real world. After years of testing products on real clients, we decided to build our own — simple, effective, and purpose-driven.

To help men look sharp, feel confident, and stay ready for whatever the day throws at them.

Crafted for the Everyday Hustle isn't a slogan — it's how we work.
